Kass' Top 10 Video Games

1. Pokemon White

Description: Pokémon Black Version and Pokémon White Version role-playing video games developed by Game Freak and published by The Pokémon Company and Nintendo for the Nintendo DS.They are the first installments in the fifth generation of the Pokémon video game series in 2010.
Kass' Thoughts: It is my first Pokemon game and what made me fall in love with the Pokemon franchise.

2. The Legend of Zelda: A Link Between Worlds

Description: The Legend of Zelda: A Link Between Worlds is action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo 3DS in 2013. The game is the 17th in The Legend of Zelda series and is a sequel to the 1991 title The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past
Kass' Thoughts: This was my first game on the 3DS handheld and one of the first Zelda games I acctually got to play.

3. The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time 3D

Description: The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time 3D is a action-adventure game developed by Grezzo and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo 3DS in 2011 with everything from the controls to the music updated from the original.
Kass' Thoughts: I have wanted to play Ocarina of time for a long time, but I do not have a Nintendo 64. I eventually found the 3DS version at a convention.

4. Minecraft

Description: Minecraft is a sandbox game developed and published by Swedish video game developer Mojang Studios in 2011. It originally created by Markus "Notch" Persson using the Java programming language, the first public build was released on 17 May 2009.
Kass' Thoughts: I like being able to explore, build and practically do what ever I want in game. I practically grew up on watching people play minecraft.

5. Wii Sports

Description: Wii Sports is a sports simulation video game developed and published by Nintendo for the Wii video game console in 2006.
Kass' Thoughts: I grew up on the Wii and on Wii Sports. The fishing game is my favorite out of the games you can play in Wii Sports.

6. Wii Sports Resort

Description: Wii Sports Resort is a sports simulation video game developed and published by Nintendo in 2009 for the Wii video game console and is the sequel to Wii Sports.
Kass' Thoughts: It is pretty much just as fun to play as Wii Sports, especially the Swordplay Showdown. Both Sports and Resort have their own charms.

7. Sonic and the Black Knight

Description: Sonic and the Black Knight is a 2009 platform game developed by Sonic Team and published by Sega as part of the Sonic the Hedgehog series for the Wii. It involves Sonic being transported to the story of King Arthur by wizard Merlina, under the pretense that they are attempting to stop a corrupted Arthur.
Kass' Thoughts: A fun play with an interesting story. It was one of the first Sonic games I ever played and has a special place in my heart.

8. Mario Super Sluggers

Description: Mario Super Sluggers is a baseball adventure video game for the Wii developed by Namco Bandai Games and published by Nintendo in 2008. It is part of the Mario Sports series, and the sequel to Mario Superstar Baseball for the GameCube.
Kass' Thoughts: It is always especially fun to play with your siblings or others in games like this.

9. Undertale

Description: Undertale is a 2D role-playing video game created by American indie developer Toby Fox in 2015. The player controls a child who has fallen into the Underground: a large, secluded region under the surface of the Earth, separated by a magical barrier.
Kass' Thoughts: I personally have not gotten the chance to physically play the game, but the story and mechanics are great.

10. Journey

Description: Journey is an indie adventure game developed by Thatgamecompany, published by Sony Computer Entertainment, and directed by Jenova Chen, on the PS3 in 2012. The player controls a robed figure in a vast desert, traveling towards a mountain in the distance. Other players can be discovered, and two players can meet and assist each other, but they cannot communicate and cannot see each other's names until after the game's credits.
Kass' Thoughts: Yet another game I have not technically been able to play, but even just watching evokes feelings and makes me think.
